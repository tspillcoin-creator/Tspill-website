Executive summary

TSPILL connects creators and vetted manufacturers to turn concepts into real products with clear attribution, straightforward royalties, and transparent economics; the token powers access, incentives, and program rails for challenges, pilots, and runs.

Problem

Creators face opaque factory access and limited share in downstream value; manufacturers need qualified briefs, predictable runs, and brand‑safe demand. Existing platforms don’t link production with attribution and ongoing rewards.

Solution

Standardized briefs and open challenges select viable concepts; winners advance to vetted manufacturing with transparent timelines and costs; attribution and simple reward rules are defined upfront and published.

How it works
  • Challenges: briefs, submissions, shortlists
  • Production: vetted factories with clear specs and lead times
  • Launch: products ship; attribution and reward rules apply
Token utility
  • Access programs and tools
  • Participation incentives for measurable progress
  • Program fees/discounts for pilots and runs
  • Signals for what to launch next
Trust & controls
