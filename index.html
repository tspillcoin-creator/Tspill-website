About

TSPILL is a production‑link network that connects creators and vetted manufacturers to turn concepts into real products with clear attribution, straightforward royalties, and transparent economics—not a funding platform.

Token function
  • Access: join open calls, submit concepts
  • Incentives: earn for useful participation
  • Program rails: simple fees/discounts for pilots and runs
  • Signals: community input on what to launch next
Ecosystem benefits
  • Creators: recognition that lasts and rewards that continue
  • Manufacturers: qualified briefs and predictable runs
  • Sponsors: transparent spend with measurable outcomes
