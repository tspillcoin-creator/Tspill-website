03 / System Intelligence

What kind of ideas belong here?

Any idea with "execution intent" that can eventually become a physical product, film, or digital tool.

How is this different from a normal store?

Every product is derived from a Validated Design that has a visible audit trail on the public ledger.

Who owns the idea?

Creators retain authorship. TSPILL acts as the production framework with transparently defined terms before execution.

Is this a competition?

No. It is an "External Review." The focus is on technical feasibility and quality over popularity.

Why the Ledger?

To ensure attribution, validation history, and revenue distribution are tamper-resistant and publicly auditable.

TSPILL moves from validation → production → participation → scale.
Each stage earns the right to exist.
