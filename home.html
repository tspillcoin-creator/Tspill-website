03 / System Intelligence

What kind of ideas belong here?

Any idea with "execution intent" that can eventually become a physical product, film, or digital tool.

Who owns the idea?

Creators retain authorship and attribution. TSPILL acts as the execution framework with transparently defined ownership terms.

Is this a competition?

No. It is an "External Review." We prioritize technical feasibility and quality over popularity.

How do royalties work?

Revenue is distributed transparently to the original creator using stablecoins or tokens based on validated design audit trails.

TSPILL moves from validation → production → participation → scale.
Each stage earns the right to exist.
